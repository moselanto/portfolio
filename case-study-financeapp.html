Sentisave Finance App

A modern finance app that transforms saving into an engaging, goal-driven experience through emotional design and gamified progress tracking.

🎯 Problem

Most financial apps focus on numbers and charts, making saving feel like a chore. Sentisave introduces emotional engagement, personalized goals, and rewarding milestones.

🧠 Approach

  • Conducted user interviews to identify pain points in saving habits.
  • Developed wireframes emphasizing progress visualization and motivation triggers.
  • Created UI prototypes that reflect warmth, simplicity, and clarity.

🌟 Result

Sentisave offers users a sense of accomplishment and motivation, turning saving into a rewarding personal journey rather than a financial obligation.
